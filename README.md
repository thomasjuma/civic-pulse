# civic-pulse

I want to build a digital civic engagement platform for the population - everyone included, the youth and women, the rich and the poor, marginalised communities, e.t.c.
The constitution of kenya has provisions for public participation in every bill tabled in parliament. These bills are the key determinants of how our institutions work and how they serve the common
man, however, a majority of the citizens do not exercise this power of public participation because of a number of reasons like lack of information on how to participate, lack of motivation,
 distance or remoteness, e.t.c. 
My aim is to encourage or motivate citizens to participate in legislative and other businesses of Parliament and its committees so as to drive openness and accountability in our
institutions for better services. My platform will help summerise and simplify notable activities in parliament like legislation and send this to the masses through email, sms, or 
any other platform like whatapp for them to read and analyses how the legislation affects them as citizens. They can then send back their reviews through the same medium and we then relay
our findings to parliament or relevant institutions for consideration. This will bridge the gap between parliament who are the drivers of our institutions and the citizens allowing
inclusivity and influence in the decisions made on thier behalf.
